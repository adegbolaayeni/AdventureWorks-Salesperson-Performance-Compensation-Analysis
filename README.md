# AdventureWorks Salesperson Performance & Compensation Analysis

**Power BI Dashboard** analyzing sales performance, quota attainment, compensation, YoY trends, and territory assignments.

## Overview
Comprehensive multi-page dashboard built in Power BI for the AdventureWorks dataset. Delivers actionable insights for sales leadership on performance tiers, compensation efficiency, and growth trends.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: $90.8M
- **Orders**: 3.8K | **Avg Order Value**: $23.85K
- **Avg Quota Achievement**: 964%
- **Total Compensation**: $1.29M | **Total Bonus**: $48.6K | **Avg Commission**: 1.18%
- **YoY Growth**: Up to 195% (top performers)
- **Territory Revenue**: North America $77M | Europe $12M | Pacific $2M

## Dashboard Pages
1. **Performance Overview** – Revenue rankings, performance tiers, growth vs decline
2. **Quota & Target Analysis** – Quota vs actuals, achievement %, gap analysis
3. **Compensation Analysis** – Total comp, revenue-per-comp, bonus & commission breakdown
4. **YoY Growth & Trends** – Growth percentages and historical comparisons
5. **Territory & Assignment** – Revenue by region/country + salesperson territory history

## Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX measures (quota achievement, YoY growth, compensation calculations)
- Data modeling (relationships between sales, quotas, compensation, territories)
- Interactive visuals: bar charts, donut charts, scatter plots, tables, treemaps

## Insights Delivered
- Top 5 salespeople generated over 50% of total revenue
- All active salespeople exceeded quotas significantly
- Identified compensation efficiency outliers and growth opportunities
